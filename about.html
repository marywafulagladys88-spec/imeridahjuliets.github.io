About

JOYATGENE ENTERPRISES is a proudly Kenyan-registered business, officially incorporated in 2021 under The Registration of Business Names Act Cap 499 Section 14 of the Government of Kenya ‘Registration Number BN-JRCGR639’. We are committed to delivering top-tier services and high-quality products tailored to meet the evolving needs and expectations of our clients.

Governance and structure

Governance and Structure – JOYATGENE ENTERPRISES operates under a clearly defined internal leadership structure, with ultimate authority vested in a Director whose roles and responsibilities are distinctly outlined.
